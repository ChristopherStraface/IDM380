Hello there, my name is Christopher Straface

I study User Experience and Interaction Design at Drexel University. I love designing beautiful, highly functional interfaces that satisfy user needs. I have a passion for helping others experience the world better through optimized UX, and this portfolio serves as an example of that passion and as a collection of what I have accomplished as a designer.

Portfolio Projects

Solo Project

PHP Developed Recipes Website

I developed from scratch a recipe website that displays over 40 recipes, which are all connected from a database using PHP. Every image, recipe title and list of instructions were added to a database and connected to this website in PHP, and given an interface with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

#UserResearch #QuantitativeSurveys #TeamCollaboration #CompetitiveAnalysis

Team Project

Food Truck Ordering App UI

In a team, I helped with the design of a prototype ordering app for the Mommy Telly's food truck. I assisted with designing the opening flow and the log-in/account creation pathway. I also assisted with UX research, continuously interviewing community members with an interest in our app, and paying close attention to their comments and feedback before moving on to our next iteration.

#UserResearch #QuantitativeSurveys #TeamCollaboration #CompetitiveAnalysis

Solo Project

UX Storytelling Animated Video

I created a UX storytelling video that follows a persona learning to navigate Instagram’s Creator tools to grow as a content creator. The project was grounded in weeks of user research and interviews, translating real creator pain points into a clear, engaging narrative that demonstrates how thoughtful UX can empower users to succeed.

#UserResearch #QuantitativeSurveys #UXStorytelling #QualitativeInterviews
